How to make chocolate

Bring a pot of water to just below a boil. Cream the cocoa powder and butter in a bowl, then add it to the pot. Sift the powdered sugar into the pot, then add the milk. Stir the mixture until it's smooth, then pour it into a mold. Let the chocolate harden, then pop it out. A more detailed description can be found at wikihow.
